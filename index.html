What we do

Your trade partner, from A to Z

We are the bridge between Egyptian factories and international buyers — one contact, full accountability.

Sourcing & partnerships

We select established Egyptian manufacturers with proven track records, audit their facilities, and negotiate competitive factory-direct pricing for our buyers.

Quality & compliance

Every shipment is inspected before it leaves Egypt. We manage certificates of origin, health certificates, and destination-market compliance so customs never surprises you.

Logistics & delivery

Sea, air, or land — we book the freight, prepare full export documentation, and track every container from Alexandria or Port Said to your door.
